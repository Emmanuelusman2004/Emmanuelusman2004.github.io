About Me

I'm Emmanuel Usman, a student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, dual majoring in Computer Science and Information Technology & Web Sciences. I am also pursuing a minor in economics.

More About Me

Coming from a Hispanic background, my parents are from Colombia.I was born soon after my family moved to the U.S. in 1999, at Elmhurst Hospital in Jackson Heights on February 12, 2004. I was raised in Queens, New York, before moving to my current hometown, Yonkers. I went to Aviation Career & Technical High School in Long Island City, where I practiced aircraft maintenance and found my passion for computing while taking an introductory CS course online.

My main goal is to earn my degree and support my family financially. I come from a big family with three brothers and one sister. One of my goals is to help my twin brother with his tuition; he's currently studying medicine at Stony Brook and aiming for med school. Knowing how expensive his career path will be, I've decided to pay my own tuition so my family can support him. After I graduate and secure a good job, I plan to assist him further so he can study comfortably without financial worries. Apart from that, I aspire to retire early and travel the world as much as possible.
